Onboarding

This is the first-use flow for the MVP.

  1. Pin the extension so you can turn reading mode on fast.
  2. Choose your CEFR level.
  3. Turn reading mode on in the popup.
  4. Select text on a page to see hard words near the text.
  5. Save useful words and review them later here.
