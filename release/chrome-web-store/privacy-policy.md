# CEFR Reading Assistant Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

CEFR Reading Assistant is a Chrome extension that helps English learners understand difficult words on webpages.

## What we collect

The extension may process:

- text that you select on a webpage
- the sentence that contains the selected word and nearby sentence context
- your CEFR setting and reading-mode preference
- words that you choose to save for review

## How we use the data

We use selected text only to provide the extension’s core feature:

- analyze difficult vocabulary in the selected text
- generate simple meanings and examples
- show vocabulary cards near the selected text
- save words locally for your review

## Where data is stored

- Your settings and saved words are stored locally in Chrome using `chrome.storage.local`.
- Selected text may be sent to the extension backend hosted on Cloudflare Workers.
- The backend may send the selected text and sentence context to OpenAI to generate structured vocabulary analysis.

## What we do not do

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not use your data for advertising.
- We do not use your data for creditworthiness, lending, or insurance decisions.
- We do not require an account for the current version.

## Data retention

- Saved words remain in local Chrome storage until you remove them or uninstall the extension.
- Backend request retention depends on the hosting and AI providers’ operational logs and policies.

## Third-party services

This product uses:

- Cloudflare Workers for backend hosting
- OpenAI for vocabulary analysis and response generation

Please review their policies if you want more information about their data handling.

## Your choices

You can:

- turn Reading mode on or off at any time
- choose what text to select for analysis
- remove saved words from the dashboard
- uninstall the extension to stop all processing
